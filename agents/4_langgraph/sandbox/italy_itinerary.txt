### 15-Day Italy Itinerary

#### Overview:
- **Total Days:** 15 days in Italy plus 2 days for travel to/from the US
- **Cities to Visit:** 5 cities: Rome, Florence, Venice, Cinque Terre, Positano.
- **Daily Schedule:** 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM for activities.

---

### Day 1: Travel to Rome
- **Departure:** Flight from the US to Rome.

---

### Day 2: Arrival in Rome
- **Morning/Afternoon:** Arrive in Rome and check into hotel.
- **Evening:** Explore Trastevere neighborhood, dinner at a local restaurant.

---

### Day 3: Rome
- **8:00 AM - 10:00 AM:** Visit the **Colosseum**.
- **10:15 AM - 12:15 PM:** Explore the **Roman Forum**.
- **12:30 PM - 1:30 PM:** Lunch nearby.
- **1:30 PM - 4:30 PM:** Visit **Vatican Museums** (including the Sistine Chapel).
- **4:45 PM - 6:00 PM:** Visit **St. Peter's Basilica**.
- **Evening:** Dinner in the vicinity of Vatican City and stroll around.

---

### Day 4: Rome
- **8:00 AM - 9:00 AM:** Breakfast.
- **9:00 AM - 10:30 AM:** Visit **Pantheon**.
- **10:45 AM - 12:00 PM:** Stroll through **Piazza Navona**.
- **12:15 PM - 1:15 PM:** Lunch.
- **1:30 PM - 3:00 PM:** Visit **Trevi Fountain** and coin toss.
- **3:15 PM - 5:15 PM:** Explore **Spanish Steps** and nearby shops.
- **Evening:** Dinner and a theatrical show (if available).

---

### Day 5: Travel to Florence
- **8:00 AM:** Depart Rome for Florence by train (1.5 hours).
- **10:00 AM - 12:00 PM:** Visit **Duomo di Firenze**.
- **12:15 PM - 1:15 PM:** Lunch.
- **1:30 PM - 3:30 PM:** Tour the **Uffizi Gallery**.
- **3:45 PM - 5:00 PM:** Stroll the **Ponte Vecchio**.
- **5:15 PM - 6:00 PM:** Explore **Boboli Gardens**.
- **Evening:** Dinner in Florence.

---

### Day 6: Florence
- **8:00 AM - 9:30 AM:** Breakfast.
- **9:30 AM - 11:00 AM:** Visit **Accademia Gallery**.
- **11:15 AM - 12:30 PM:** Explore **Mercato Centrale** for local eats.
- **12:45 PM - 1:45 PM:** Lunch.
- **2:00 PM - 5:00 PM:** Day trip to **Siena**, explore the square and cathedral.
- **Evening:** Return to Florence for dinner.

---

### Day 7: Travel to Venice
- **8:00 AM:** Train to Venice (2 hours).
- **10:00 AM - 12:00 PM:** Check into hotel and explore **St. Mark's Basilica**.
- **12:15 PM - 1:15 PM:** Lunch.
- **1:30 PM - 3:30 PM:** Visit **Doge's Palace**.
- **3:45 PM - 4:45 PM:** Walk across the **Rialto Bridge**.
- **5:00 PM - 6:00 PM:** Gondola ride.
- **Evening:** Dinner with a view.

---

### Day 8: Venice
- **8:00 AM - 9:30 AM:** Breakfast.
- **9:30 AM - 12:00 PM:** Day trip to **Murano and Burano Islands**.
- **12:15 PM - 1:15 PM:** Lunch on Burano.
- **1:30 PM - 4:30 PM:** Free time in Venice for shopping and exploring.
- **Evening:** Attend an opera/concert at **Teatro La Fenice**.

---

### Day 9: Travel to Cinque Terre
- **8:00 AM:** Train to Cinque Terre (3 hours).
- **11:00 AM - 2:00 PM:** Check into accommodation and relax at **Monterosso al Mare** beach (swimming).
- **2:15 PM - 6:00 PM:** Explore **Vernazza**.
- **Evening:** Dinner overlooking the sea.

---

### Day 10: Cinque Terre
- **8:00 AM - 9:00 AM:** Breakfast.
- **9:00 AM - 11:00 AM:** Hiking from **Monterosso to Vernazza**.
- **11:15 AM - 1:15 PM:** Explore **Corniglia**.
- **1:30 PM - 2:30 PM:** Lunch in Corniglia.
- **2:45 PM - 5:45 PM:** Visit **Manarola** and **Riomaggiore**.
- **Evening:** Dinner in Monterosso.

---

### Day 11: Travel to Positano
- **8:00 AM:** Train and bus to Positano (5 hours).
- **1:00 PM - 4:00 PM:** Check in, have lunch, and relax on the beach.
- **Evening:** Dinner in Positano with a sunset view.

---

### Day 12: Positano
- **8:00 AM - 9:00 AM:** Breakfast.
- **9:00 AM - 12:00 PM:** Boat to **Amalfi**, explore **Amalfi Cathedral**.
- **12:15 PM - 1:15 PM:** Lunch in Amalfi.
- **1:30 PM - 4:30 PM:** Visit **Ravello** (Villa Rufolo).
- **Evening:** Attend a local festival or event.

---

### Day 13: Positano
- **8:00 AM - 9:00 AM:** Breakfast.
- **9:00 AM - 12:00 PM:** Relax at **Fornillo Beach** (swimming and sunbathing).
- **12:15 PM - 1:15 PM:** Lunch.
- **1:30 PM - 4:30 PM:** Visit local shops and cafes in Positano.
- **Evening:** Dinner by the beach.

---

### Day 14: Return to Rome
- **8:00 AM:** Travel back to Rome (3 hours).
- **11:00 AM - 2:00 PM:** Last-minute shopping/sightseeing in Rome.
- **2:15 PM - 3:00 PM:** Lunch.
- **Evening:** Last dinner in Rome.

---

### Day 15: Departure
- **Morning:** Travel back to the US.
